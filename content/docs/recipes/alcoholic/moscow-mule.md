# Moscow Mule

## Ingredients

- 2oz (60ml) vodka
- Juice from half a lime
- 4oz (120ml) ginger beer

## Steps

1. Add vodka to a collins glass with ice
2. Squeeze in fresh lime juice
3. Top with ginger beer
4. Garnish with a lime wedge
