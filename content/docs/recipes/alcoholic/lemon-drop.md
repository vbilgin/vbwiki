# Lemon Drop

## Ingredients

- 2 oz (60ml) vodka
- .75 oz (45ml) fresh lemon juice
- .75 oz (45ml) simple syrup

## Steps

1. Combine all ingredients into a shaker tin with ice
2. Shake and then double strain into a chilled coupe glass
3. Garnish with a lemon twist
